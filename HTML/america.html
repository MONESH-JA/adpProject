Grand Canyon, Arizona

What to do: Explore the vast, stunning canyon through hiking, rafting, or scenic viewpoints. You can also take a helicopter ride for an aerial view. Why visit: One of the most iconic natural wonders in the world with breathtaking views and hiking trails.

New York City, New York

What to do: Visit landmarks like Times Square, Central Park, the Statue of Liberty, the Empire State Building, and the Metropolitan Museum of Art. Why visit: A vibrant city that never sleeps, with endless cultural, shopping, and dining experiences.

Yellowstone National Park, Wyoming/Montana/Idaho

What to do: See geysers like Old Faithful, wildlife like bison and bears, and stunning landscapes such as Yellowstone Lake and the Grand Prismatic Spring. Why visit: The first national park in the world, famous for its geothermal features and natural beauty.
