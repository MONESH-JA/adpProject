PLACE TO VISIT

RED SQUARE

Things to See and Do

  • Walk around the square and take photos of the stunning architecture.
  • Visit the Kremlin, a UNESCO World Heritage site.
  • Admire the beautiful St. Basil's Cathedral and its intricate designs.
  • Learn about Russia's history at the State Historical Museum.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Red Square is during the spring and summer months (May to September) when the weather is mild. However, winter offers a magical atmosphere with snowy views of the square and the Kremlin.
