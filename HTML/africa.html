Cape Town (South Africa)

Mountain: A stunning flat-topped mountain offering panoramic views of the city and surrounding coastline. You can hike or take a cable car to the top. V&A Waterfront: A vibrant area with shopping, dining, and entertainment options. It also offers boat trips to Robben Island, where Nelson Mandela was imprisoned. Kirstenbosch National Botanical Garden: A beautiful garden set against the backdrop of Table Mountain, showcasing South Africa's unique plant life.

Victoria Falls (Zimbabwe & Zambia)

One of the largest and most spectacular waterfalls in the world, Victoria Falls lies on the Zambezi River, marking the border between Zimbabwe and Zambia. The falls are a UNESCO World Heritage Site and offer activities like bungee jumping, white-water rafting, and scenic helicopter flights. The nearby Zambezi National Park and Mosi-oa-Tunya National Park offer excellent wildlife viewing.
