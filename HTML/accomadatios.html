Luxury Hotels


The Carlton Kodaikanal: Overlooking the beautiful Kodaikanal Lake, this hotel offers elegant rooms, a spa, and fine dining options.
Hotel Hilltop: Nestled amidst lush greenery, this hotel provides luxurious amenities and stunning views of the hills.
Mid-Range Hotels
Kodai Resort Hotel: Known for its warm hospitality, this resort features comfortable rooms and is close to major attractions.
Hotel Green Palace: Offers a mix of comfort and affordability, with spacious rooms and a nice garden.
Budget Accommodations
Hostel Kodaikanal: A cozy option for backpackers, this hostel provides dormitory and private rooms at budget-friendly rates.
Zostel Kodaikanal: A popular choice among young travelers, offering a vibrant atmosphere and various activities.
Homestays
Ganga Homestay: Experience local culture with a stay in a traditional homestay, offering home-cooked meals and personalized service.
Cottage in the Woods: Perfect for a peaceful getaway, this homestay is surrounded by nature and provides a cozy ambiance.
Tips for Booking
Seasonal Considerations: Kodaikanal is popular year-round, but booking in advance is crucial during peak seasons (summer and holidays).
Local Attractions: Consider accommodations close to key attractions like Kodaikanal Lake, Coaker's Walk, and Bryant Park for convenience.
